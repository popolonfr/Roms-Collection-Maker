La Corona Encantada (final) is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=71E4252E
crc32=01342ACB
SHA1=75a59677275602e9a2b314fc077411b23754308f
SHA1=b5bb76d666dda2c3ad93f2ece44be72f5d0f61e3
