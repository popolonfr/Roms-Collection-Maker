Shoulder Blade Overdrive (v1.0) is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=88CE879B
SHA1=349fc714c0c19b7cd749f75e618fffb6c2855fcb
