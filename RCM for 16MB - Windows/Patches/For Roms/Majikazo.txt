Majikazo is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=EA6B647D
crc32=39B8F033
SHA1=0c52d589f0c8e7e778a61d7f4561f37316a5fa63
