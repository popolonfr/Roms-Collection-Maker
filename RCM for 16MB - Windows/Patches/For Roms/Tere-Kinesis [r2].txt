Tere-Kinesis (r2) is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=A2B4FF26
crc32=4263351A
SHA1=90424f20cf92d52129d6c0f6cb78075d24917981
SHA1=dac7aa7aa8f286caabd7f0e53d4734803c5120ac
