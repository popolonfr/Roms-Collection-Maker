Alter Ego is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=41B51564
crc32=DD934878
SHA1=0cf251004caf6d874a66b05e100b599e5eaf9d32
