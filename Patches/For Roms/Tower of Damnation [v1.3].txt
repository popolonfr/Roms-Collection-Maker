Tower of Damnation (v1.3, 50/60Hz) is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=4FADD800
