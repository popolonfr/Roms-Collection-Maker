The Cure (MSX support fix version) is a 48kB plain ROM. This patch modifies the Rom to be executable from the Popolon's flash Rom SCC cartridge in multi-Rom mode.

Use the ROM with a checksum from those listed below to apply this patch.

crc32=D5A83BA4
